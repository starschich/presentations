\sectionslide{The transmission bottleneck}{Why the grid can't keep up with industrial demand}

\begin{frame}{Built for large industrial loads}
  America's electricity network was not designed for gigawatt-scale,
  point-to-point industrial demand. We're building one that is.

  \vspace{0.8em}
  \begin{itemize}
    \setlength{\itemsep}{0.6em}
    \item \textbf{Long-haul efficiency} --- HVDC moves power across the
          continent with far lower losses than AC.
    \item \textbf{Controllable flow} --- direct control over how much power
          goes where, and when.
    \item \textbf{Multi-terminal} --- a true DC network, not just isolated
          point-to-point links.
  \end{itemize}
\end{frame}
