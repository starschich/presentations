\intermezzo{AC circuit breaker}{}

\begin{frame}{Operation principle}
  \begin{columns}[T,onlytextwidth]
    \begin{column}{0.56\textwidth}
      Their main purpose is to clear any fault that might occur in the
      HVDC system.

      \vspace{0.6em}
      \textbf{Operation principle:}
      \begin{itemize}
        \setlength{\itemsep}{0.35em}
        \item Rapidly separate two contacts surrounded by a liquid or
              gaseous insulating medium.
        \item Arcing period until the next zero crossing of the current.
        \item Arc is extinguished as quickly as possible.
        \item After interruption a transient recovery voltage (TRV) appears
              across the open contacts.
      \end{itemize}

    \end{column}

\end{frame}
