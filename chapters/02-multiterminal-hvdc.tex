\sectionslide{Multi-terminal HVDC, end to end}{A true DC network, not point-to-point links}

\begin{frame}{How it works}
  A multi-terminal DC network connects many sources and loads through a
  shared DC backbone, with power electronics controlling flow at every node.

  \vspace{0.8em}
  \begin{itemize}
    \setlength{\itemsep}{0.6em}
    \item \textbf{DC $\rightarrow$ DC} --- 525\,kV backbone linking terminals.
    \item \textbf{Deterministic control} --- route gigawatts on demand.
    \item \textbf{Scalable topology} --- add terminals without rebuilding the core.
  \end{itemize}
\end{frame}
